About me

I am a sociologist with an MSc from the University of Oxford and a BA from the University of Iceland, with a strong background in qualitative and quantitative research, data analysis, and academic writing. Currently I work at Alda, an Icelandic software company, in research and development — covering topics ranging from gender equality to software development and micro-learning. I am fluent in Icelandic and English, highly proficient in Swedish, and have a working knowledge of Danish and Norwegian.
