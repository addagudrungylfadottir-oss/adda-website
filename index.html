About me

I am a sociologist with an MSc from the University of Oxford and a BA from the University of Iceland. I combine a strong research background — qualitative and quantitative analysis, surveys and academic writing — with hands-on content and learning design. At Alda, an Icelandic software company, I lead research and content: I built the micro-learning content process from the ground up and grew the library to nearly 70 modules, contributing to Alda being named Educational Startup of the Year 2025. I am fluent in Icelandic and English, highly proficient in Swedish, and have a working knowledge of Danish and Norwegian.
